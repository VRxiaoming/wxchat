Hospital confirmed a leader in MDT software use

Update: Mon May 16, 2016

 

FAHZU has stood out as a national leader in application of information technology with its MDT (Multidisciplinary Team) software copyright having been registered by the China National Copyright Administration.  

The registration procedure was completed earlier in March, but it was confirmed on Saturday that FAHZU is so far the first and only hospital in China that has registered an MDT software copyright.

MDT is an advanced medical approach, in which doctors from different disciplines work together to address patients' complex clinical needs.

FAHZU set up its MDT center in July 2013. Up to now, the center has provided services to more than 7,000 patients who suffer malignant tumors, and emergency and severe diseases.

To further improve the efficiency to benefit more patients, the Hospital developed and perfected a computer software to manage and coordinate its MDT work. The software was formally put into clinical use in June last year.

China gives priority protection to registered software in order to promote the development of its software industry. The copyright owner enjoys right of publication and right of authorship, among other rights.
